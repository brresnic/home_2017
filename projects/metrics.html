Metrics

Quantitative User Research

I spent seven months as the quantitative user researcher for IBM's Analytics Platform portfolio. The details of that work aren't public. But, at a high level, my role was to determine IBM's strategy for utilizing usage metrics across a portfolio of SaaS products, and then implement that strategy.

I worked with Meghan Corbett, another user researcher, to develop a comprehensive framework for IBM product teams to integrate quantitative and qualitative user research. We built off of existing frameworks including Google HEART and IBM's universal experiences. I showed Offering Managers how they could improve their work by utilizing metrics. I implemented a software instrumentation pipeline, utilizing Segment.io. I worked on personalizing user experiences through data-driven triggers.
